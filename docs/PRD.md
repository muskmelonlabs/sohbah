\# Sohbah - Product Requirements Document (PRD)



\---



\## 1. Product Overview



Sohbah is an Islamic mentorship platform that connects learners with mentors for personalized guidance in Arabic and Islamic topics.



Unlike traditional platforms, Sohbah focuses on:

\- Human connection

\- Flexible learning

\- Topic-based guidance (not rigid courses)



\---



\## 2. Vision



To become the go-to platform where Muslims around the world learn Islam through meaningful human connections.



\---



\## 3. Problem Statement



Non-Arab Muslims face major challenges:

\- Cannot understand Arabic

\- Struggle with Quran recitation and meaning

\- Lack access to approachable mentors

\- Feel disconnected from structured learning systems



Existing solutions:

\- Too rigid (courses, madrasa systems)

\- Too transactional (tutoring platforms)

\- Lack mentorship and relatability



\---



\## 4. Solution



A platform where:

\- Learners discover mentors

\- Learn specific topics on demand

\- Build meaningful learning relationships



\---



\## 5. Target Users



\### 5.1 Learners

\- Non-Arab Muslims (India, Pakistan, Indonesia, etc.)

\- Beginners to intermediate learners



\*\*Goals:\*\*

\- Learn Arabic basics

\- Improve Quran reading

\- Understand Salah and daily practices



\---



\### 5.2 Mentors

\- Native Arabic speakers

\- Students of knowledge

\- Individuals strong in fundamentals



\*\*Note:\*\*

Not limited to scholars — approachable mentors are key.



\---



\## 6. Platform Type



Mentorship Marketplace + Learning Platform
